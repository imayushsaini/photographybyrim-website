About

Established in 2016 by a group of enthusiasts, RIM 4.0 is the photography club of MIET which has gone through various transitions since it's inception.
With the initial intent still alive, RIM 4.0 is a club that creates memories.
   Covering all the events and occasions at our college, RIM 4.0 has always been focused on providing the best roll for job. Whether you see them or not, they're always working, seizing those moments which make you re-live the moments.
Over the span of 3 years, RIM has grown to expand from capturing photos and videos to photo editing, graphic designing, portfolio shoots and video editing.
Members of all ages work together in this family of artists, organising it's own open to all photowalks, workshops and events like Enfoque under the umbrella of GAL, including SEYAHAT, the first of it's kind photo exhibition which was a great success at Kolaahal 2019.
